Services

ANY PETS

The whole team of the veterinary clinic is happy to welcome you

We have been welcoming you since 2002 and since then we have constantly modernized our clinic to offer you a space entirely dedicated to the well-being of your companions. But equipment is nothing without a high-performance team, and because veterinary medicine is evolving like human medicine, we have chosen to diversify our skills to provide your animals a complete care service. Techniques also evolve very quickly: we regularly invest in new equipment to stay at the cutting edge of progress, and provide your companions with the means best suited to their needs.

Our services

Quality care for the well-being of your animals

Consultations

Annual consultations and vaccinations, to check the state of health of your animal and to detect possible pathologies. So-called "general" or more "specialized" consultations when your animal is sick. Consultations are by appointment.

Imaging

Digital radiography, Doppler scanner, Endoscopy. As in humans, these images allow us to detect trauma invisible to the naked eye.

Hospitalization

Two hospital kennels are available. One is reserved exclusively for animals suspected of or suffering from a contagious infectious disease; the other makes it possible to take care of patients who are sick, injured, convalescing or in post-operative wake-up monitoring.

Emergencies

In an emergency, call the on-call service who will send you the number and contact details of the on-call veterinarian by courier. We are on duty on average two evenings a week and every other weekend. We do not provide home emergencies.

Nutrition

Health depends above all on a good diet, balanced and adapted to both age, your pet's lifestyle and health. Do not hesitate to ask us for advice during your next visit..

Hygiene and care

We have a wide range of products to take care of your animals: Dental care, ear care, shampoos, dewormers, fleas, ...

Education

Our veterinarians and auxiliaries give you advice and tips for the education of your pets. In the event of abnormal and repeated behavior, we guide you in order to put an end to these actions.
